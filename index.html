About Me

Hello! I'm Adam Alvarez, a Data Scientist and Project Manager in Raleigh, NC. With a background spanning data analytics, digital marketing, and software project management, I'm passionate about using data to navigate our complex world.


My education includes an undergraduate degree in Business Management from University of Virginia's McIntire School of Commerce, and a Data Science degree from BloomTech. My technical toolkit includes Python, SQL, Excel (including VBA), and various machine learning frameworks. My professional values are built on a framework of integrity, hard work, and effective collaboration. I embrace challenges, uphold honesty, and value diverse perspectives to foster innovation and achieve shared goals.


Outside of work, I enjoy cooking, wine, travel, and adventuring outdoors with my wife, daughter, and dog. My goal is to become a domain expert in data science, helping companies thrive in a data-driven economy.


Whether you're interested in collaboration or just connecting, I'd love to hear from you. Let's explore how we can use data to make sense of the world together!
